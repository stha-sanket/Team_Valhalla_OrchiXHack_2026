\section{Notable Choices}
\textbf{\code{ws} over \code{socket.io}}: the pathfinder needs a single
bidirectional stream with no rooms, namespaces, or polling fallback; raw
WebSocket is a quarter of the bundle size and makes the ticket-based
upgrade authentication straightforward.
\textbf{RTK~Query over React~Query}: it ships inside Redux Toolkit at zero
extra dependency and makes cross-feature cache invalidation (confirm a
visit \(\rightarrow\) refresh progress, points, and leaderboard) declarative.
\textbf{\code{jose} over \code{jsonwebtoken}}: ESM-native and actively
maintained, matching the fully \code{"type": "module"} server.
\textbf{BullMQ for email}: SMTP delivery takes 200--2000\,ms; queueing it
keeps registration and password-reset responses immediate.
\textbf{Vendored AR libraries}: version pinning and immunity to ad-blockers
and CDN failures, loaded on demand so the initial bundle stays small.
